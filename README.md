### âœ… Key Findings (Short Summary)

* Longer movies (>120 minutes) tend to receive better ratings.
* Adult certificate movies increased in recent decades.
* Most movies fall under the "Very Good" rating category.
* The most repeated themes in descriptions include words like **life, young, two, help, family**.
* Movie production peaked during the 2000s decade.
